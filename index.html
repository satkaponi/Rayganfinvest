    Statement of purpose

    Considering the trends in the last several years and which are further accentuated by the recent Corona Virus pandemic, the country needs talent to help and strengthen the leadership in MSME’s and guide them for performance improvement and growth. While the MSME’s are fully aware of what help they require, it is very clear that they do not have either the mindset nor the resources to spend on high fixed salaries on high-capability talent. The government and economists know that MSME’s require a high level of management expertise and intervention to make them efficient, productive, and cost leaders, transforming them into becoming employment generators, highly profitable and world class.



    The preference of serious, and hands-on consulting practitioners will become the trend by companies, especially MSME’s, whereby they can hire high levels of expertise at a fraction of their annualized market rates. Moreover another advantage of employing consultants is that they will be engaged for temporary periods, therefore resulting in variabilized costs rather than fixed cost., which implies that they are in the nature of ‘pay-per-use’ and ‘are discontinued’ post achievement of objectives.



    Raygan Finvest LLP offers a hands-on, practical, work-together methodology (“WTM” Copyright) of consulting where targeted outcomes are clearly defined, realistic, and with focus on execution.



    The Client shall have the flexibility of choosing the levels of engagements of the work-together plans. This depends on the level of skills and bandwidth available within the organization.



    We follow the Japanese philosophy of step-by-step approach instead of ‘offering the moon’ by some international consultants. This assures achievable and sustainable improvements.
